Let's Play

To play, simply enter the codes on your game pieces and press the button. They will automatically be filled in on the digital gameboard. Remember to use the button and not the Enter/Return key on your keyboard! Also be sure to enter the codes exactly as they appear on the gameboard and gamepiece, without any spaces, lowercase letters, or punctuation of any kind. These characters will cause the system to fail in recognizing your game piece code.

Please note that upon leaving the page, your session will be lost. Keep this in mind before navigating to any of the other pages on the website or leaving your session unattended!
